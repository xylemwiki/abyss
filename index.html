Dominion Abyss

Dominion Abyss is the 16th expansion of Dominion published by Xylem Games in March of 2024. Dominion Abyss has 31 Kingdom cards, and introduces 12 Marine Cards. Abyss is the first set with Marine Cards and follows an underwater and oceanic theme. The expansion icon for abyss is .
